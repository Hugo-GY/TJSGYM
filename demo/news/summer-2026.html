Summer 2026

Our Tiddler class for 6-12 months returns to the programme in April.

TJ’s Gymnastics Club is bringing back its Tiddler class for babies aged 6 to 12 months from April.

The class returns as a gentle way for parents and babies to enjoy movement, play, and early physical development in a bright and supportive environment.

Booking options include pay-as-you-go at £10 per class, or a Tiddler card for six weeks at £54, giving families a little flexibility depending on what suits them best.

The people behind the magic

Meet Our Coaches

A passionate, close-knit team — many of whom started at TJ's as children themselves. Together we bring over a century of gymnastics experience to every class.

The lead team

Heading Up the Club

Gill Holland
General Manager & Head Coach

Gill founded TJ's in September 1988 and has been running it for 36 wonderful years. Holds Level 3 qualifications in both General and Pre-school Club Coaching.

Level 3 · General & Pre-school
Natalie Garlick
Head of Gymnastics & SEN Co-ordinator

Coached by Gill as a teenager, Natalie has been part of the team since 1997. She leads competitions, the display team, and is our Special Educational Needs Co-ordinator.

Level 3 · General & Pre-school
Jade Shaw
Deputy Head Coach

Jade first came to TJ's aged one in Toddler Gym and progressed all the way to squad — the ultimate TJ's story! Her two daughters, Kyla and Nyrah, attend the club too.

Level 2 UKCC · Pre-school Add-on
Nelum Samara
Safeguarding & Welfare Officer

Coached by Gill during her school days, Nelum reconnected via social media years later and joined as Welfare Officer in September 2024 — thrilled to be back in the sport she loves.

Name: Gill Holland

Bio:
Gill is the General Manager of T.J’s Gymnastics Club, and heads the Pre school programme. Her involvement in gymnastics began in childhood, progressed to coaching and, after graduating in 1988 with a BSc (Hons) in Sports Studies, she began coaching as a career. TJ’s opened in September 1988, September 2024 is Gill’s 36th year running TJ’s!! It is fantastic having some of the gymnasts from a few years back now bringing their children!

Gill holds both her Gymnastics and Pre-school Club Coach Level 3 coaching certificates required by the British Gymnastics Association to run classes. Gill is married to Chris and they have two young adult sons, Max and Sam. In August 2012 she was diagnosed with breast cancer, so had to take a year out of coaching for the treatment, but returned back to coaching in Sept 2013. Now it’s all a distant memory and enjoying being back to her old self!

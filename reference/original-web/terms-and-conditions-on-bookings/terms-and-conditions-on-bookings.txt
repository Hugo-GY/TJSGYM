Terms and Conditions on Bookings
https://www.tjsgymclub.co.uk/terms-and-conditions-on-bookings/

– Our classes are run on a termly basis. The classes are coached, structured classes, the equipment set ups become more challenging as the term progresses as learning occurs.

– If we have spaces, and consideration is given to the above statement, children can start mid term.

– We prefer that bookings are made for the term. If, after a few, weeks you feel the class is not right for your child, we are happy to refund remaining classes. We want to work with you and your child to give them the best possible experience at TJ’s and we know that it can take a few weeks for the children to get to know us and what to expect. This is why we prefer bookings to be made for the term.

– No trial classes will be given for the first two weeks of term.

– If we have spaces, we can offer a trial class. However, consideration must be given to the fact there is a lot to see and do and, for some, the first class can be quite intense! It can take a few weeks for children to settle into the classes, the first class is not always the best guide for how your child will enjoy our classes. Our classes are progressive, equipment set up and skills become more demanding as the term progresses. For this reason, we would prefer to offer a discounted rate for children starting after the start of term rather than a trial class. But we are happy to offer trials from 3rd week of term, so long as the above is understood.

– We cannot offer refunds or ‘make up’ classes for those missed. However, in serious medical circumstances, we can make exceptions to this rule.

– Half term bookings is an option in exceptional circumstance, please do contact Gill to discuss.

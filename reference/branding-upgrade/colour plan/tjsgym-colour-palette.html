Typography Proposal

Recommended direction for a cuddly, parent-friendly, mobile-first TJSGYM site

Recommended Font Pairing

This proposal aims for warmth and approachability first. The heading font feels soft and playful without becoming childish, while the body font stays clean and easy to read on phones.

Headings: Fredoka
Body: DM Sans
Tone: cuddly, friendly, confident, family-oriented
Why not Playfair: too editorial and formal for this brand direction

Live Preview

A happy, confident place for little gymnasts to grow

Fredoka gives headings a rounded, welcoming voice that feels much more aligned with a child-focused club and parent audience.

DM Sans works well for schedules, programme details, FAQs, booking flows, and mobile reading. It stays clear at smaller sizes, feels modern without being cold, and supports trust and ease of use. Together, Fredoka and DM Sans create a warmer, softer, more approachable identity than a Playfair Display based system.

Soft Card Panels

Rounded corners, generous padding, light pink surfaces, and soft separation instead of heavy borders.

Friendly CTAs

Primary actions use pill buttons with bright magenta or deep plum and simple, parent-friendly wording.

Check Availability

Buttons, Cards, Navigation

  • Primary actions should feel clear and reassuring, not sales-heavy.
  • Cards should use soft surfaces, `12-20px` radius, and generous padding.
  • Navigation should stay simple, uncluttered, and booking-first.
  • Desktop and mobile both need an obvious path to classes, timetable, and booking.

Forms, Imagery, Layout

  • Booking forms must be touch-friendly, calm, and easy to scan on mobile.
  • Use warm, joyful, real photography wherever possible.
  • Decorative details should stay soft and controlled, never gimmicky.
  • Layouts should feel open and breathable, with hierarchy driven by spacing and colour.

Name: Natalie Garlick

Bio:
Natalie has also been involved in gymnastics since childhood, was coached by Gill as a teenager and then, as a young adult she began to assist coaching at TJ’s. She has been working at T.J’s since 1997, gaining her Level 3 qualifications in General and Pre-school Club Coach. Natalie is married to Paul, their eldest, Jack, will be 19 in July ’24 and Lucy will be 17 in March ‘24.

Natalie heads the General Gymnastic programme, being the lead coach in the competitions we enter and the display team and is our Special Needs Co-Ordinator.

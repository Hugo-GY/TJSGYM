Club News

Congratulations!

Both Natalie and Jade recently passed their Level 3, Module 1 assessment, marking an important step in their ongoing coaching development.

The British Gymnastics assessor gave some extremely good feedback and said TJ’s “is such a wonderful unique club that is incredible”.

We’ll take that. It is a strong endorsement of the atmosphere, standards, and care that make the club feel special to the children and families who come through it every week.

About This Class

Progressive gymnastics for children aged 5+

Our Gymnastics classes are for children aged 5 and over who have moved up through TJ's pre-school programme. Sessions are built around floor and vault work, helping gymnasts develop confidence, technique, body control and strength in a structured but encouraging environment.

We use our own badge progression system, moving from Levels 7 to 1 and then on to Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum and Diamond awards. Classes are popular and our venue is small, so most children join this programme after attending Mini Gym in the Summer term before they start Reception.

Going Further

Competition & Displays

For gymnasts who want to go further, we offer competition and display opportunities throughout the year. These are entirely optional — never a requirement — and are open to children at appropriate levels in our Gymnastics programme.

Our annual end-of-year display is a highlight of the TJ's calendar: a chance for every gymnast to perform in front of family and friends in a supportive, celebratory atmosphere. It's a proud moment for gymnasts and parents alike.

  • Annual end-of-year club display — open to all Gymnastics classes
  • Local and regional competitions for those who want them
  • Squad-level preparation for gymnasts pursuing higher levels
  • Always optional — we celebrate participation at every level

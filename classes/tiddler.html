About This Class

Parent & Baby movement, music and play

Tiddler Gym is designed to offer a fun, safe environment for parent or carer and baby — encouraging physical play and interaction through music, rolling, rocking, crawling and balancing. The equipment is carefully chosen for the Tiddler age range: tactile, bright and engaging for young explorers.

The circuit changes every single week, so there's always something fresh to discover. It's also a lovely chance for parents and carers to relax, have a chat, and watch their little ones thrive.

Important Information

Terms and Conditions on Bookings

Our Tiddler class is our only class offering Pay as You Go. Booking can be made via the website and also at the class. So, no need to book in advance, just come along. We do also offer the option of a Tiddler Card, payment for 6 sessions which is then stamped each time you come and is slightly cheaper than paying for each class individually.

If you are a regular member of Tiddlers you will have priority to book the Toddlers class when your child is ready to progress.

We cannot offer refunds or ‘make up’ classes for those missed. However, in serious medical circumstances, we can make exceptions to this rule.

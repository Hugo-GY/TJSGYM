# Impeccable Design Context
## TJ's Gymnastics Club — Website v2

---

## Design Context

### Users
Parents and carers in South West London (Wimbledon, Raynes Park) looking for gymnastics classes for their children aged 6 months to 16+ years. They're on mobile as much as desktop, often in a hurry, and need to quickly find the right class for their child's age. First-time visitors compare options; returning visitors want to check timetables and book. Trust and warmth are essential — this is a community club, not a corporate gym.

### Brand Personality
**Warm. Playful. Trustworthy.**
- Cuddly and reassuring — never corporate or clinical
- Community-rooted — 35+ years of local history
- Inclusive and encouraging — every child can belong here

### Aesthetic Direction
- Palette: Deep Plum #7A2A7A (anchor), Magenta #D94A9F (CTAs/energy), Blush #F3C7E7, Pink Wash #F7E9F5, Mint #39C39A (sparingly), Lime #DDEB3C (highlight accent)
- Typography: Fredoka 500 for headings (soft, rounded, warm), DM Sans 300–600 for body
- Rounded corners everywhere: 12–24px for cards, 999px for pills and buttons
- Body background: #FFF9FD with subtle plum dot pattern (22px grid)
- Light mode only
- Reference aesthetic: Pupbar — organic, playful, generous spacing

### Design Principles
1. **Consistency first** — every page shares the same header, footer, section-label pattern, and spacing scale. No custom layouts that break the system.
2. **Components, not one-offs** — use `.card-accent`, `.card-feature`, `.btn-ghost-magenta`, `.cd-booking-table`, `.section-label` etc. Never reinvent these.
3. **Breathing room** — generous padding (var(--space-4xl) vertical rhythm on sections). Never cramped.
4. **Typography hierarchy** — Fredoka headings in plum/magenta, DM Sans body in text-body/text-light. `<em>` inside h1/h2 is always magenta.
5. **Warmth in details** — magenta bullet dots, blush borders, plum-tinted shadows. Everything feels hand-crafted and inviting.

### Key Component Patterns (must copy exactly)
- **Header**: `.site-header > .nav-wrap > [logo-link img] + [nav-overlay div] + [nav-links] + [nav-toggle button with 3 spans]`
- **Footer**: `.site-footer > [footer-watermark span] + [footer-inner 3-col grid] + [footer-bottom]`
- **Page Hero** (inner pages): `.page-hero > .container > [page-hero-eyebrow] + h1 (plum, em=magenta) + [page-hero-sub]` — white background, NOT a dark gradient
- **Section header**: `[section-label span] + h2` — label is small magenta uppercase, h2 is plum Fredoka
- **Tables**: `.cd-booking-table` style — white bg, magenta uppercase headers (centered), blush borders, pink-wash hover rows
- **Buttons**: `.btn-ghost-magenta` is the standard site CTA button
- **CTA Band**: `.timetable-cta` / `.cta-band` — plum background, white text, white + ghost-white buttons

🌟 6–12 Months

Tiddler Gym

A parent-and-baby activity class designed to encourage physical play and interaction between parent and baby. The tactile, bright and stimulating environment changes every week so there's always something fresh to explore.

  • Music, rolling, rocking, crawling and balancing activities
  • New circuit layout every week — always something fresh
  • Informal social time for parents and carers
  • No term commitment — pay as you go, or save with a 6-class card

🌈 1–2¾ Years

Toddler Gym

An active exploration class for toddlers with full parent and carer participation. Structured sessions build confidence, coordination and gross motor skills through guided equipment circuits — with a calm cool-down to close.

  • Warm-up using action songs — listening and copying skills
  • Weekly changing equipment circuit supervised by coaches
  • Maximum class sizes to ensure individual attention
  • Calming cool-down songs to close each session

🤸 3–4½ Years

Mini Gym

An independent class where children begin learning the foundations of gymnastics. Parents observe from the doorway, with a dedicated watching week at the end of each term. Groups are kept small for maximum focus.

  • Rolling, handstands, springboard, vault and simple bar work
  • Max 5 children per coach for focused individual attention
  • Theme-based weekly curriculum — skills build progressively
  • British Gymnastics membership required (£18–£24/year)
£154 Spring term · 11 sessions

🏅 5+ Years

Gymnastics

Our main programme for children who have progressed from Mini Gym. A progressive badge scheme takes gymnasts from Level 7 through to Diamond, with competition and display opportunities for those who want them.

  • Floor and vault focus — strength, flexibility and technique
  • Progressive badge scheme: Level 7 → Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum → Diamond
  • Beginners, Intermediate and Squad levels available
  • Competition and display programme for interested gymnasts
From £154 per term · level dependent

Good to Know

Before You Book

♿

SEN Inclusive

We are able to accommodate SEN children and would be happy to discuss what we can offer. Please contact us before booking to arrange a suitable introduction session.

🏅

British Gymnastics Registered

All coaches are members of British Gymnastics. Children joining Mini Gym or Gymnastics classes must hold a BG membership (£18–£24/year) — we'll help you set this up.

👥

Small Class Sizes

Numbers in every class are strictly limited — and assistant coaches are provided where necessary. This ensures every child receives the focused, quality coaching they deserve.

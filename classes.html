For gymnasts who want to go further, we offer competition and display opportunities throughout the year. These are entirely optional — never a requirement — and are open to children at appropriate levels in our Gymnastics programme.

Good to Know

Booking Terms

A few things to understand before you book your child's place.

Our classes run on a termly basis. We prefer bookings are made for the full term, as sessions are coached and structured — the equipment and challenges build progressively as the term goes on. If spaces are available we can accommodate children starting mid-term, but this is at our discretion.

Trial classes are available from the 3rd week of term onwards (not the first two weeks). We'd ask you to bear in mind that the first class can feel quite intense — it can take a few weeks for children to settle in. For children joining after the start of term, we often prefer to offer a discounted rate rather than a single trial, so your child gets the chance to really find their feet.

We don't offer refunds or make-up classes for missed sessions. However, if after a few weeks you feel the class isn't right for your child, we are happy to refund remaining classes — we want to work with you to find the best experience for your child. In serious medical circumstances, we can make exceptions to our refund policy.

Half-term bookings are available in exceptional circumstances. Please contact Gill directly to discuss your situation — we'll always try to find a solution that works.

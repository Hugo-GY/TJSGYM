Our Programmes

Classes for Every Stage

From crawling tiddlers to confident gymnasts — we have a class designed for your child's development.

🌟 6–12 Months

Tiddler Gym

A fun parent-and-baby environment using music, rolling and crawling.

  • Tactile, bright equipment changed every week
  • Music and sensory play throughout
  • Parent-and-baby together — no drop-off
Explore Tiddler Gym
🌈 1–2¾ Years

Toddler Gym

Action songs, climbing, and a large weekly circuit to explore.

  • Large circuit with varied equipment
  • Coaches lead — parents assist
  • Fun weekly themes and action songs
Explore Toddler Gym
🤸 3–4 Years

Mini Gym

Independent themed sessions — children work in small groups of five.

  • Rolling, handstands and springboard
  • Bar work and structured progressions
  • First independent class — no parents on floor
Explore Mini Gym
🏅 5+ Years

Gymnastics

Our main programme with floor, vault and apparatus for older children.

  • Own badge scheme: Level 7 to Diamond
  • Floor, vault and apparatus work
  • Structured competition pathway available
Explore Gymnastics
